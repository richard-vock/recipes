yield: 4
prepTime: 5min

ingredients:
- 2-3 onions
- a half bunch of parsley
- 1-2 tbsp of sumach
- 1 tsp of cumin
- salt
- olive oil
- lemon juice

directions:
- Cut onions into thin strips.
- If onions are too harsh soak in cold water for half an hour and strain afterwards.
- Add finely cut parsley, sumach and cumin.
- Season with salt, olive oil and lemon juice according to taste and rest of the dish.
---

A great twist on plain, cut onions. Common in Turkish *Döner Kebab* dishes but can be used in any salad context.
