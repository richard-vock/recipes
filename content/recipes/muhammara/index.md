ingredients:
- 4 red bell peppers
- 150g walnuts
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 tbsp pomegranate sirup
- 1 chile
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 50ml olive oil
- more olive oil (to cover surface)

directions:
- Bake peppers at 220°C until they start to blacken (roughly an hour) and let them cool in the oven for half an hour.
- Remove seeds and pith and blend with the rest of the ingredients.
- Once transferred to the serving dish, cover with olive oil.
---

A delicious paste of grilled bell peppers and walnut, this slighlty sweet mezzeh fits perfectly into any buffet. Note that the
pomegranate sirup provides the sourness, not sweetness. If you absolutely *have* to replace it, provide something sour like lemon/lime juice.

I pretty much always prepare this dish together with [Baba Ghanoush](/recipes/baba_ghanoush) so that I can bake the eggplant together with the bell peppers.
