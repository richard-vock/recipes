category: Sauce
cuisine: French
tags:
  - Cream
  - Classic
yield: 2
prepTime: 10min

ingredients:
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp wheat flour
- 250ml milk
- nutmeg
- optionally cheese (like parmesan)

directions:
- Liquify butter in pan at medium heat.
- Sprinkle flour onto butter and mix.
- Heat until flour taste is gone (with some experience you can smell it) but before it starts to get brown.
- Stir in the milk as slowly as possible.
- Finally add muskeg and cheese to taste.
---

A perfect example of the French cuisine's perfection in hiding fat under the rug of elegance.

This is also an example of something so easily and quickly done that it makes absolutely no sense to ever buy it pre-made.

Try variations of more heavily roasted Roux (the heated flour-butter mix) if you fancy.
