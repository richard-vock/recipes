ingredients:
- 250g wheat flour
- 62% water
- 1.2% yeast
- 2% salt

directions:
- Mix and kneed until gluten structure forms -- dough should have a temperature of 25°C.
- Stretch and fold after 30min.
- Pre-shape after another 30min.
- During final 20min proof pre-heat oven with baking sheet to 250°C without fan.
- Stretch and roll out to 0.5-1cm thick elongated strips. Use minimal flour and pressure.
- Bake strips one by one on baking sheet. They should puff up nicely -- remove when slightly browned.
