yield: 8
prepTime: 10min
cookTime: 60min

ingredients:
- 1 Kg (i.e. roughly 4) eggplants
- 10-12 tbsp Tahini (roasted sesame paste)
- 10 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1-2 tsp salt
- olive oil (to cover surface)

directions:
- Cut 4 roughly 1cm deep cuts into each eggplant.
- Bake eggplants on a sheet for an hour at 220°C.
- Let eggplants cool down for a short while.
- Depending on desired consistency use a fork, a mortar or an immersion blender
  to mix the eggplant's pulp with the other ingredients.
- Once transferred to the serving dish, cover with olive oil.
---

If you haven't decided yet whether or not you like eggplants, try this tasty mezzeh made from grilled eggplants
and tahini. A great contribution to any buffet.

I pretty much always prepare this dish together with [Muhammara](/recipes/muhammara) so that I can bake the eggplant together with the bell peppers.
