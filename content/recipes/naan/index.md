yield: 4
prepTime: 5min (90mins rest time)

ingredients:
- 300g wheat flour (you can substitute a third by whole grain wheat flour)
- 65% warm water
- 2% yeast
- 1% salt
- 0.5tsp sugar
- garlic oil for coating

directions:
- Rehydrate yeast by mixing it with some of the water and the sugar.
- Wait for yeast to foam.
- Separately mix all dry and all liquid ingredients (except for the coating ones).
- Mix everything until a smooth (quite wet) mass is formed.
- Cover the mixing bowl with cling film or a wet towel and wait one to one and a half hours (until mass has doubled in size). Occasionally stretch-and-fold with wet hands.
- Using a little flour to handle the dough, divide it into 4 equal pieces and roll out into 20-25cm diameter discs.
- Fry in a oil-covered pan until lightly browned on both sides.
- Using a brush coat with garlic oil and serve as desired.

A soft, flat bread that is very versatile in how you use it. Quite similar to Greek Pita bread.

This is so easy and quick to make that it is my goto bread to use as a side dish for basically anything you would alternatively combine with rice or potatoes.
Or spread some [Hummus](/recipes/hummus) on it and enjoy.
