ingredients:
- 1 onion
- 2 banana peppers
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 4 tomatoes
- chiles according to taste
- a bunch of parsley
- 1 tbsp of pepper paste
- 1 tbsp of tomato paste
- 1 tbsp pomegranate sirup
- some olive oil and salt

directions:
- Mix all ingredients in a food processor - you want everything cut thinly, not blended to a paste.
- Strain water by putting resulting mix in a fine sieve over a pot/bowl for at least half an hour.

For some a vital ingredient in fast food style *Döner Kebab*, Ecili Ezme is a hot, yet fresh paste ideally combined
with a minty yogurt sauce.

Can also be used as a spread on hot summer days.
