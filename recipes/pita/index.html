Pita Bread

Classic pita bread – ideal for filling.

  • Yields: 4
  • Preparation time: 10min (2 hours total)
  • Sources: Chainbaker: Text version | Video version.

Ingredients

  • 250g wheat flour
  • 62% water
  • 1.2% yeast
  • 2% salt

Steps

  1. Mix and kneed until gluten structure forms – dough should have a temperature of 25°C.
  2. Stretch and fold after 30min.
  3. Pre-shape after another 30min.
  4. During final 20min proof pre-heat oven with baking sheet to 250°C without fan.
  5. Stretch and roll out to 0.5-1cm thick elongated strips. Use minimal flour and pressure.
  6. Bake strips one by one on baking sheet. They should puff up nicely – remove when slightly browned.
