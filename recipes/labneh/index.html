Labneh

Sometimes called “Labneh cheese” this thick, smooth jogurt mezzeh paste is a great spread or dip and my favorite contribution to any buffet.

Use it everywhere you would use a fresh cream cheese with summer herbs.

  • Yields: 8
  • Preparation time: 5min (rest overnight)

    Ingredients

    • 1 Kg jogurt
    • 1 tbsp salt
    • 0.5l water
    • olive oil (to cover surface)
    • mint
    • garlic powder (fresh also works)

    Steps

    1. Optionally sanitize the cloth by pouring boiling water over it and waiting for 10 minutes.
    2. Mix jogurt, water and salt (tf. essentially making Ayran).
    3. Put a sieve on a pan, then spread a cloth inside the sieve and fill with jogurt mix.
    4. Let everything sieve over night in the fridge.
    5. Next day use a spoon to scrape the resulting paste off of the cloth and transfer to a large enough vessel.
    6. Mix in mint and garlic according to taste.
    7. Cover surface with olive oil and serve anytime.
    8. If fresh garlic is used wait at least half an hour for the garlic aroma to unfold. Otherwise it can be served directly.
