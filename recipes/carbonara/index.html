Spaghetti Carbonara

The classic Italian dish properly done. No cream cheese, no bells, no whistles. Perfect for a late night cooking session as the fancyness-to-complexity ratio is unmatched.

  • Yields: 1
  • Preparation time: 2min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Sources: Video by J. Kenji López-Alt

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • bacon, diced
  • 1 egg (possibly 2, but then only the egg yolk of the second)
  • 2-3 tbsp parmesan
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salt water.
  2. Meanwhile roast bacon in olive oil and remove the pan from heat to cool down.
  3. Whisk egg and parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Once the spaghetti are just shy of al-dente, transfer them directly to the bacon pan. Taking some starchy salt water with them is intended.
  5. Add egg mix to the pan and mix everything thoroughly. It is important that the remaining heat in the pan is not sufficient to have the egg curdle and thicken.
  6. Add salt and pepper to your liking. Herbs are fine too but definitely not a must.
