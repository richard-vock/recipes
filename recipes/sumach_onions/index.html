Sumach Onions

A great twist on plain, cut onions. Common in Turkish Döner Kebab dishes but can be used in any salad context.

  • Yields: 4
  • Preparation time: 5min

Ingredients

  • 2-3 onions
  • a half bunch of parsley
  • 1-2 tbsp of sumach
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. Cut onions into thin strips.
  2. If onions are too harsh soak in cold water for half an hour and strain afterwards.
  3. Add finely cut parsley, sumach and cumin.
  4. Season with salt, olive oil and lemon juice according to taste and rest of the dish.
