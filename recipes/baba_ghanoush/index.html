Baba Ghanoush

If you haven’t decided yet whether or not you like eggplants, try this tasty mezzeh made from grilled eggplants and tahini. A great contribution to any buffet.

I pretty much always prepare this dish together with Muhammara so that I can bake the eggplant together with the bell peppers.

  • Yields: 8
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 60min

    Ingredients

    • 1 Kg (i.e. roughly 4) eggplants
    • 10-12 tbsp Tahini (roasted sesame paste)
    • 10 tbsp lemon juice
    • 2 cloves of garlic
    • 1-2 tsp salt
    • olive oil (to cover surface)

    Steps

    1. Cut 4 roughly 1cm deep cuts into each eggplant.
    2. Bake eggplants on a sheet for an hour at 220°C.
    3. Let eggplants cool down for a short while.
    4. Depending on desired consistency use a fork, a mortar or an immersion blender to mix the eggplant’s pulp with the other ingredients.
    5. Once transferred to the serving dish, cover with olive oil.
