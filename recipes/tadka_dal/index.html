Tadka Dal

While originating in a pretty hot country this common dal variant is the perfect example of a warming winter dish for me. Serve with Naan.

  • Yields: 4
  • Preparation time: 2min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Sources: Serious Eats

Ingredients

  • piece of ginger (5-10cm based on size)
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 8 tablespoons of ghee
  • 2 onions
  • 1/2 tsp of cayenne
  • 1/2 tsp of smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground tumeric
  • 300g red lentils
  • 2 tsp of (preferably black) mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp of cumin seeds
  • 2 green thai chiles, stemmed and sliced

Steps

  1. Add half of ghee to medium heat large pot, add onions and cook until brown. Stir often.
  2. Puree ginger and garlic with 50ml of water.
  3. Add puree, cayenne, paprika, coriander, ground cumin and tumeric and cook until water is gone.
  4. Add one liter of water and lentils, bring to boil and then simmer for 20 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  5. In a separate small pot/pan roast mustard seeds, cumin and chiles in the remaining ghee. Add to final dish when serving.
