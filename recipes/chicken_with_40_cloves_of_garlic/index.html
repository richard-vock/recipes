Chicken with 40 cloves of garlic

A simplified and slightly modified version of the NYT cooking recipe. You can find multiple versions online - important is the interplay of the garlic paste, the vermouth and the tarragon. The rest merely sets a stage for these and invite to experimentation. Also try vegetables like potatoes or brussel sprouts in addition to or instead of chicken.

  • Yields: 2
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 90min

    Ingredients

    • pieces from one chicken (use spare parts to make broth)
    • olive oil
    • 40 cloves of garlic (unpeeled)
    • 3-4 celery ribs
    • 3 onions
    • 150ml of vermouth
    • 6 parsley sprigs
    • 1tsp of dried tarragon
    • optionally thyme, rosemary, etc.
    • 1 tbsp salt
    • 0.5 tsp nutmeg
    • pepper

    Steps

    1. Pre-heat oven to 190°C.
    2. Coat chicken pieces in olive oil.
    3. In a heavy casserole put one layer of celery, onions, parsley, tarragon and other herbs
    4. Put chicken pieces on top
    5. Pour vermouth and add salt, pepper and nutmeg
    6. Stuff cloves of garlic between all the pieces
    7. Bake for 90 minutes without removing the lid
    8. Serve with roasted baguette or ciabatta and squeeze garlic cloves onto the bread while eating.
