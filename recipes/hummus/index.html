Hummus

My absolute favorite thing to spread onto bread. This one tends to be spicy as it is – in contrast to the bland stuff usually sold in grocery stores in this part of the world. However, feel free to adjust to your liking – a litte bit more tahini or garlic never hurt anyone…

  • Yields: 8
  • Preparation time: 15
  • Cooking time: 5

    Ingredients

    • 1 can (250g) of chick peas
    • 3 tbsp tahini
    • 1-2 tsp lemon juice
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 3 cloves of garlic (or 0.5 tbsp powdered garlic if necessary)
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1 pinch of cayenne pepper
    • salt
    • parsley, cut (optional)
    • olive oil (to cover surface)

    Steps

    1. Drain liquid from chick peas can into another container (you need that later).
    2. Add all other ingredients except parsley and mix.
    3. Blend everything using an immersion blender while adding the saved chick pea liquid to achieve the desired consistency. If you don’t know your preferences yet add just enough to achieve a smooth surface after blending.
    4. Transfer to target vessel, cover with olive oil and sprinkle parsley on top (paprika or cayenne works too for decoration). Should last a day or two.
