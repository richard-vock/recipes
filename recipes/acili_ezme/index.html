Ecili Ezme

For some a vital ingredient in fast food style Döner Kebab, Ecili Ezme is a hot, yet fresh paste ideally combined with a minty yogurt sauce.

Can also be used as a spread on hot summer days.

  • Yields: 8
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Sources: Multiple recipes (in German): kein Stress kochen

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 banana peppers
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tomatoes
  • chiles according to taste
  • a bunch of parsley
  • 1 tbsp of pepper paste
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp pomegranate sirup
  • some olive oil and salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a food processor - you want everything cut thinly, not blended to a paste.
  2. Strain water by putting resulting mix in a fine sieve over a pot/bowl for at least half an hour.
