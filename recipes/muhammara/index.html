Muhammara

A delicious paste of grilled bell peppers and walnut, this slighlty sweet mezzeh fits perfectly into any buffet. Note that the pomegranate sirup provides the sourness, not sweetness. If you absolutely have to replace it, provide something sour like lemon/lime juice.

I pretty much always prepare this dish together with Baba Ghanoush so that I can bake the eggplant together with the bell peppers.

  • Yields: 8
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 60min

    Ingredients

    • 4 red bell peppers
    • 150g walnuts
    • 2 cloves of garlic
    • 1 tbsp pomegranate sirup
    • 1 chile
    • 0.5 tsp salt
    • 50ml olive oil
    • more olive oil (to cover surface)

    Steps

    1. Bake peppers at 220°C until they start to blacken (roughly an hour) and let them cool in the oven for half an hour.
    2. Remove seeds and pith and blend with the rest of the ingredients.
    3. Once transferred to the serving dish, cover with olive oil.
